## Direct Answer Pattern

```markdown
## [Main question]

[Answer in 40-80 words. Be specific, accurate, and usable without the rest of the page.]

In practice:

- [Key point 1]
- [Key point 2]
- [Key point 3]

Important nuance: [limitation, exception, or decision criterion].
```

## Content Audit Checklist

- Intent: Does the page answer the real query behind the keyword?
- Direct answer: Is there an extractable answer near the top?
- Structure: Are headings question- or decision-oriented?
- Originality: Does the page add experience, data, examples, or proof?
- Trust: Are author, sources, dates, claims, and limitations clear?
- SEO basics: Title, meta, H1, indexability, internal links, mobile readability.
- AEO readiness: Follow-up questions, entity clarity, citation-worthy sections, third-party proof.
- Brand fit: Is this brand SEO, non-brand SEO, or a mixed-intent page?
- CTA: Does the next step match user readiness?
- Maintenance: What should be updated and when?

## 30-Day SEO/AEO Action Plan

### Week 1: Audit
- Verify Search Console and indexing.
- Identify top business pages and queries.
- Separate brand and non-brand demand.
- Check technical blockers and mobile usability.

### Week 2: Research
- Map target topics to questions.
- Review SERP and answer surfaces.
- Identify cited competitors and third-party sources.
- Prioritize 5-10 opportunities.

### Week 3: Content
- Optimize 3-5 existing pages.
- Add direct answers, proof, internal links, and FAQ.
- Create 1-3 missing high-value pages.

### Week 4: Authority and Measurement
- Set SEO and AEO KPIs.
- Track target questions across answer surfaces.
- Identify mention/citation opportunities.
- Plan distribution through owned and third-party channels.
