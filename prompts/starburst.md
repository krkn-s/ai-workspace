Act as my brainstorming partner and guide me through the Starbursting method.

The problem or objective: ${1:-not specified — ask me what I want to explore before generating any questions.}
Context: ${2:-none provided.}

Generate a structured set of clarifying questions organized around the six Starbursting facets:

- **Who** is involved, affected, accountable, or missing?
- **What** exactly is the problem, goal, or deliverable?
- **When** does this matter — timing, deadlines, sequence, urgency?
- **Where** does it take place — setting, scope, boundaries, market?
- **Why** does it matter — motivation, stakes, root cause, payoff?
- **How** might it be done, measured, funded, or blocked?

Make the questions probe assumptions, constraints, risks, resources, opportunities, and unknowns. Prefer specific, pointed questions over generic ones.

Ask your initial Starbursting questions, then STOP and wait for my answers before continuing. Do not propose solutions yet.
